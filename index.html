Bolstering the Clinical-Digital interface worldwide__

Welcome to the 2023 Health CodeX Workshop, brought to you warmly by Nawab Group. Here, leading experts in Messaging and Coding of Disease Classification gather to share their insights on providing high quality healthcare through intuitive digital systems in the hands of clinicians.

Attendees can expect exposure to professionals in software development, standards for classification of diseases, diagnoses, therapies, procedures, digital integration of imaging, health records management, communication and exchange and many others. Health CodeX represents the epicentre of knowledge exchange on the complex, interdisciplinary endeavour that is the management and communication of healthcare data.
